Redemption Policy

You may choose redeemable, non-redeemable, or hybrid NFTs. A recommended redeemable flow:

  1. Holder opens a redemption request via the website with KYC and shipping details.
  2. Holder burns the NFT on-chain or marks it as redeemed via contract call.
  3. Issuer ships 1 g of C60 to verified address under agreed logistics and fee schedule.
  4. Redemption fee covers shipping, handling, and verification; recipient signs receipt on delivery.

Warning: physical transfers require compliance and safety procedures. Specify how redemption handles hazardous shipping rules if applicable.

FAQ

What does the NFT represent?
Each NFT is a digital certificate representing 1 gram of physically stored C₆₀ fullerene held in our vault and verifiable via batch documentation.
Can I redeem for physical fullerene?
Yes if the token is redeemable. Redemption requires identity verification, payment of shipping/handling, and burning or marking the token as redeemed.
How do NFTs interact with FUL60 tokens?
NFTs are certificates. FUL60 is a divisible ERC-20 representation (1 mg per token). NFT revenue can be used to seed FUL60 liquidity pools; NFTs do not automatically convert to FUL60 tokens unless specified in the sale terms.
